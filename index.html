About Me

My name is Daniel Claudio. I was born in the Philippines and currently live in Quezon City. I enjoy watching nature documentaries, inspiring movies, and meaningful series that teach valuable life lessons and help me see the world from different perspectives. My goal is to continue developing my abilities so that I can contribute positively to my career, my family, and the community around me.
